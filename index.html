Process

How It Works

Every drop goes through our curation process, ensuring only the finest Asian music makes it onchain as limited NFTs.

01 / Artist
Submit
Artists from across Asia apply to have their music curated on Synthlab. We review sound, story, and intent.
02 / Curation
Curate
Our team hand-selects each drop. Limited editions, strong narratives, and authentic sound — every time.
03 / Mint
Mint NFT
Music is minted as a limited NFT on Solana. Collectors own a real piece of the art and its revenue stream.
04 / Earn
Revenue Split
Artist, collector, and platform share in perpetual royalties. Enforced onchain — transparent and automatic.

Legal Notice

All music rights remain with respective artists. NFTs represent shared ownership and revenue participation rights — not copyright transfer. Use of this platform constitutes agreement with our terms of service. Please ensure compliance with your local regulations regarding digital assets and NFTs. This platform is not available in jurisdictions where music NFTs are prohibited.
